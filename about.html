The Dynamic Duo

Photography brought us together, but it's our friendship that has kept us that way.

7 years ago, at a church we were both members of, we bonded over our shared passion for documenting life with our cameras.

We would spend hours talking about photography, helping, advising, critiquing, and cheering each other on as we pursued our individual photography businesses

The conversations soon turned to be about more than just our cameras and before we knew it, we were best friends...helping and cheering each other on in everything, not just photography.

In 2016, after 6 years of friendship and individual photography businesses, after hiring one another for our individual weddings, helping each other at our individual sessions, we decided to officially join forces, taking on the world of wedding photography as a team. What makes us different than other wedding photographers? Our friendship...which we invite all of our clients to be a part of when they trust us with documenting their love story.

This friendship is what sets Naty & Jenn apart. Not only are you hiring two women with cameras...you are hiring two halves of a whole, a dynamic duo that was born from a passion for documenting life. The love that we have for our friendship and our craft is reflected in the experience we provide all of our couples with...we hope that you'll let us provide that for you.

 

Naty

I was born in Brasil, but have been in Texas for 11 years. It was here in Texas that I met the two loves of my life - photography and my husband. I have been a photographer for 7 years, have been married for 9, and am a proud mommy of twin boys.

I have been the owner of Natalia Florencio Photography for 7 years, specializing in families, children, birth, and lifestyle photography. When I am not behind my camera, you can find me editing images, cooking or cuddling with my family, Netflix-ing, shopping too much on Amazon, or drinking coffee.
